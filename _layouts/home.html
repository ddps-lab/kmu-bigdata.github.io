Bigdata Lab in Kookmin Univ.

The BigData Laboratory (BDLAB) is part of the Department of Computer Science at the Kookmin University, Seoul. The BDLAB is interested in a broad range of topics including cloud computing, serverless computing, performance management, large-scale data analysis, and machine learning.
